The Album Experience

Enrich & monetise music superfans with one virtual product.

What is a Storyline?

Storylines are virtual products that live on the Alta app. They pull exclusive pictures, video and text into one slide based experience.

Why is it valuable for Artists?

Sold to fans individually, each Storyline is quick to create, lucrative & scalable. A better way to tell your core story to your most important fans.

How does it work?

Decide on the story you want to tell, work with us to create a structure and send it off to be designed. Set a release date for our app and bring your journey to life.
